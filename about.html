About

In May 2025, I created a website called, "Ventura County Line Dancing". It was, "a passion project of mine when I got the idea of creating an all in one place for line dancing info relating to Ventura County and the hobby itself". Now just over a year later in July 2026, I have learned a lot from creating the website and am creating SoCal Line Dancing.

Goal

My goal with this website is to share info about line dance venues and more around Southern California. With improved features and broader information, I hope that this website helps even more people line dancing. As always, hope to see you on the dance floor!
